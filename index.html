Biography

Paschalis Giakoumoglou is a Research Assistant in Media Analysis, Verification and Retrieval Group (MeVer) of the Information Technologies Institute at the Centre for Research and Technology Hellas (ITI-CERTH). His research interests include Time Series Forecasting, Deep Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Image Forensics and more.
